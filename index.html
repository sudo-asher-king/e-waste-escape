◈ THE STORY ◈

Year 2145. Earth has become a graveyard of electronic waste.

Giant corporations abandoned billions of broken devices, poisoning oceans, soil and air with toxic chemicals.

You are NOVA — a young scavenger and repair engineer who joins the underground resistance: THE GREEN CIRCUIT.

Nova discovers an ancient RECYCLING CORE capable of restoring the planet — but only if you can:

  • ♻ Collect and recycle electronic devices
  • 🔧 Repair broken technology
  • ⚡ Defeat the giant pollution machines
  • 🌍 Restore balance to the ecosystem

The fate of Earth is in your hands.
